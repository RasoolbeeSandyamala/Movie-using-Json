About Telugu Movies

Guntur Kaaram

Mahesh Babu's Guntur Kaaram is one of the most anticipated films of 2024. Directed by Trivikram Srinivas, this action-packed entertainer promises to deliver powerful dialogues, intense action scenes, and Mahesh Babu in a larger-than-life role. Alongside him, Sreeleela and Meenakshi Chaudhary add star power to the film. Known for his ability to balance mass appeal with family drama, Trivikram is expected to bring a mix of high-voltage entertainment and emotional depth to this film, set against a vibrant backdrop. The music, composed by Thaman S, adds energy and excitement to the narrative.

Saindhav

Starring Venkatesh in the lead, Saindhav is a gripping action thriller. Directed by Sailesh Kolanu, the movie also features Bollywood actor Nawazuddin Siddiqui and Shraddha Srinath in pivotal roles. Venkatesh plays a dynamic character in a tale filled with crime, vengeance, and intense drama. Set in a dark, suspenseful atmosphere, Saindhav stands out for its unique storyline and strong performances. The inclusion of Nawazuddin Siddiqui, a notable actor from Bollywood, adds further intrigue to the narrative, blending Southern and Northern film industries.

Naa Saami Ranga

Naa Saami Ranga brings together Nagarjuna Akkineni and Allari Naresh in an action entertainer that appeals to mass audiences. Directed by debutant Sridhar Reddy, the film follows a story of revenge and loyalty, where Nagarjuna's character faces formidable challenges. Allari Naresh plays a significant role, adding layers of emotion and humor to the film. With strong action sequences and a compelling storyline, this movie aims to be a hit among fans of Nagarjuna's action-oriented films. The film's blend of intense action and family drama is expected to connect well with audiences.

Pushpa 2: The Rule

The sequel to the blockbuster Pushpa: The Rise, Pushpa 2: The Rule continues the journey of Allu Arjun's character as he navigates the complex world of red sandalwood smuggling. Directed by Sukumar, the film has raised high expectations with its larger-than-life portrayal of a rugged anti-hero who refuses to back down. Rashmika Mandanna reprises her role as Srivalli, and Fahadh Faasil's villainous character adds tension to the narrative. With stunning visuals, intense drama, and a powerful storyline, Pushpa 2 is set to be one of the biggest Telugu releases of 2024, with fans eagerly awaiting its action-packed sequences and memorable dialogues.

Kalki 2898 Ad

Kalki 2898 AD, a sci-fi extravaganza, stars Prabhas alongside Deepika Padukone, marking one of the most ambitious projects in Indian cinema. Directed by Nag Ashwin, this futuristic film promises to take viewers on a journey through time and space, set in a distant future where technology and mythology blend seamlessly. Prabhas plays a larger-than-life character in a visually stunning world, with Deepika Padukone's character adding depth and intrigue. With extraordinary special effects and a high-budget production, Kalki 2898 AD is poised to be a groundbreaking film, pushing the boundaries of Telugu and Indian cinema.
